AI voice concierge

An expressive, realistic AI host

The AI voice experience is meant to sound like a polished host, not a flat assistant. In a production version, this would use a conversational voice stack with pauses, emphasis, warmth, and quick turn-taking for phone and on-site web voice interactions.

Reservations + ordering

Real guest flows with AI handling the conversation

The reservation experience is integration-ready, which matters because the restaurant’s public OpenTable page indicates it is not currently on the OpenTable booking network. The AI therefore needs to work with OpenTable, Resy, SevenRooms, Tock, Yelp Guest Manager, or a custom booking layer rather than assuming one live provider.

Can I get Saturday at 7:00 PM for four?
We’re fully booked at 7:00 PM for four, but I can offer 6:15 PM, 6:30 PM, or 8:00 PM. I can also place you on the waitlist or help with pickup or delivery tonight.
Let’s do 8:00 PM. It’s for an anniversary.
Perfect — I can hold 8:00 PM for four and add your anniversary note. Please confirm your name and mobile number for the text confirmation.
I want delivery: General Tso’s Chicken, one Dynamite Roll, and fried dumplings.
Absolutely. I have General Tso’s Chicken, 1 Dynamite Roll, and Fried Pork Dumplings. Would you like to add a Japanese Soda or Kani Salad? Once you confirm, I’ll collect your delivery address and provide the estimated arrival time.
